About Me

When I'm not making art you might find me on a walk with my dog, or enjoying a cup of tea.

I received a BFA in Painting and Ceramics at the University of Texas at Arlington in 2009.

I currently reside in beautiful Seattle, WA.

If you would like to contact me about artwork prices, project collaborations, or shows you may do so at lisaeddington@gmail.com.
